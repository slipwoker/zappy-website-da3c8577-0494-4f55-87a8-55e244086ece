Questions, Answered

What IT services do you provide for small businesses?

We offer comprehensive managed IT services including network setup and maintenance, cybersecurity solutions, cloud infrastructure, data backup and recovery, hardware procurement and support, and 24/7 monitoring. Our team handles everything from initial consultation to ongoing technical support, ensuring your technology infrastructure runs smoothly.

What information should I prepare before your visit?

Please have ready: a list of current devices and software, any existing network diagrams or documentation, login credentials for systems we'll be working on, and a clear description of any issues you're experiencing. If you have specific goals or pain points, documenting those helps us tailor our approach to your needs.

How long does a typical service visit take?

Most on-site assessments take 2-4 hours, depending on your infrastructure complexity. Simple troubleshooting might be resolved in under an hour, while full network installations can span multiple days. We'll provide a time estimate after our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Can you work remotely or do you need to be on-site?

Many services can be handled remotely through secure connections—software updates, monitoring, troubleshooting, and configuration changes. However, hardware installations, network cabling, and initial setup typically require on-site presence. We'll recommend the most efficient approach for your specific situation.

How do you ensure our business data stays safe?

We implement multi-layered security: encrypted backups, firewall configuration, antivirus deployment, regular security audits, and staff training on best practices. While no system can guarantee absolute immunity from threats, we follow industry-standard protocols to minimize risk and ensure rapid response to any incidents.

What happens after the initial service visit?

You'll receive a detailed report of work completed, recommendations for improvements, and documentation of your updated systems. We offer flexible ongoing support packages—from pay-as-you-go to comprehensive managed services. Our team remains available via phone, email, and remote access for any follow-up questions or issues.

Have a question we didn't cover? Contact us and we'll provide the answers you need to make the right decision for your business.
